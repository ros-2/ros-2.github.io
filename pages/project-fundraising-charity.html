Fundraising Automation

Multi-million pound charitable enterprise

Date: Nov 2025
Build Time: 2 hours
Saves: 6.5 days/year

Goal: Identify repetitive manual tasks within the organisation and automate them to reduce costs and save time, allowing staff to focus on higher-value work.

Grant applications took a full day each: finding funders and filling out repetitive forms with the same charity information.

I built a system that stores the charity's information and creates draft forms. Input project needs, get matching funders and pre-filled applications.

Eight hours reduced to ten minutes. Six forms per year saves 6.5 working days.
