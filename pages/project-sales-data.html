Sales Data Restructuring

Multi-million pound charitable enterprise

Date: Dec 2025
Build Time: 20 hours
Saves: 14 days/year

Goal: Identify repetitive manual tasks within the organisation and automate them to reduce costs and save time, allowing staff to focus on higher-value work.

Sales data was trapped in 43,000 formulas across multiple sheets. Staff ignored most data because it took too much effort to understand.

I rebuilt the database and moved all calculations to Looker Studio. Staff now use clickable filters to find data instantly.

Two hours per week saved. Non-technical staff now pull insights in minutes instead of struggling through complex sheets.
