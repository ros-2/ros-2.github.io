Workflow

Simple, transparent process

1. Free Discovery Call (30 minutes)

We discuss your current processes and pain points. I ask questions about what's consuming staff time and identify automation opportunities. You'll leave with a clear understanding of what could be automated and estimated time savings. No obligation to proceed.

What happens: Remote video call at a time that suits you. Bring examples of repetitive tasks or processes that frustrate your team.

2. Scoping & Build (1-3 days typically)

If we proceed, I spend focused time understanding your workflows in detail and building the automation. For complex projects, I'll visit on-site for a day or two. For simpler automations, I can work remotely with occasional check-ins.

What you get: Regular updates on progress, opportunities to review and provide feedback, and a working system built to your specifications.

Timeline: Most projects complete within 1-2 weeks from start to finish, depending on complexity and your availability for feedback.

3. Delivery & Handover

You receive the completed automation with full documentation explaining how it works. I provide training sessions for your team so they understand how to use and maintain the system. You're not left dependent on me.

Support included: Follow-up support to resolve any issues and ensure the system is delivering the expected time savings. Minor tweaks and adjustments included in the first month.

What you won't get

  • ✗ Lengthy contracts or retainer requirements
  • ✗ Jargon-heavy technical documentation
  • ✗ Systems that only I can maintain
  • ✗ Hidden costs or scope creep
  • ✗ Over-engineered solutions
