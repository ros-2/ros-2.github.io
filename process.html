What I Offer

Services

I automate repetitive business processes and build custom solutions for data processing, workflow orchestration, and systems integration. Projects include grant application automation, production tracking, EPOS data consolidation, and expense processing. I also provide business research and feasibility analysis for new ventures.

Services include discovery workshops to identify automation opportunities, full implementation with documentation, and staff training for smooth adoption.

How It Works

Process

1. Free Discovery Call

We discuss your current processes and pain points. I ask questions about what's consuming staff time and identify automation opportunities. You'll leave with a clear understanding of what could be automated and estimated time savings. No obligation to proceed.

What happens: Remote video call at a time that suits you. Bring examples of repetitive tasks or processes that frustrate your team.

2. Free Problem Identification (1-2 Days)

If we decide to move forward, I spend 1-2 days with your team identifying the problems and mapping your workflows in detail. This is completely free - you only pay if we proceed to implementation. I'll document the bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and automation opportunities, giving you a clear roadmap even if you choose not to proceed.

What you get: Detailed analysis of your processes, identification of high-impact automation opportunities, and a clear implementation plan with estimated time savings.

3. Project Delivery

Once we've identified the problems and agreed on the scope, I build the automation solution. You receive the completed system with full documentation explaining how it works, plus training sessions for your team so they understand how to use and maintain it.

Pricing: Fixed project-based pricing agreed upfront. No hourly billing or surprises. You know exactly what you'll pay before work begins.

Support included: Follow-up support to resolve any issues and ensure the system is delivering the expected time savings. Minor tweaks and adjustments included in the first month.
