Grant Writing Automation

Pre-fill grant applications with your organisation's information, saving hours on repetitive forms

The problem

Grant applications take a full day each: finding funders and filling out repetitive forms with the same charity information.

Every application asks for the same core information about your organisation, your mission, your financials, and your governance. Yet teams spend hours copying and pasting this information into different forms, reformatting text, and ensuring consistency across applications.

The solution

This system stores your charity's information and creates draft grant application forms automatically. Simply input your project needs, and the system will match you with relevant funders and generate pre-filled applications.

The system:

  • Stores your organisation's core information in one central location
  • Matches your project requirements with suitable grant funders
  • Automatically fills out application forms with the relevant information
  • Maintains consistency across all your applications
  • Creates draft applications ready for your review and customisation

Results

Eight hours reduced to ten minutes per application. For organisations submitting six grant applications per year, this saves 6.5 working days annually.

The automation handles the repetitive data entry, freeing your team to focus on the unique aspects of each application: tailoring your project description, highlighting relevant outcomes, and crafting compelling cases for funding.

Who is this for?

This system is ideal for:

  • Charities and social enterprises applying for multiple grants each year
  • Organisations with limited fundraising capacity
  • Teams spending significant time on grant administration
  • Charities wanting to apply for more grants without increasing staff hours

How it works

The system is built specifically for your organisation:

  1. Setup: We capture your organisation's core information, including mission, governance, financials, and standard project descriptions
  2. Customisation: We configure the system to match the grant applications you most commonly complete
  3. Use: When you need to apply for a grant, input your project requirements and the system generates a pre-filled draft application
  4. Review: You review and customise the application before submission
