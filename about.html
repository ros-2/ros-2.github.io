About Philip Ross

I'm an engineering graduate from the University of Strathclyde with a track record of delivering automation that actually works. My background combines technical expertise with hands-on business experience, which means I understand both the code and the operational reality.

Before launching this consultancy, I worked at an AI analytics startup serving major FMCG companies. I led testing for LLM-powered tools and built Python automation that compressed days of data analysis into minutes. This taught me how to build reliable systems under real business constraints.

I specialise in practical automation: Google Apps Script for spreadsheet systems, Python for data pipelines, and AI tools like Claude and custom GPTs for intelligent processing. I choose technology based on what will be maintainable for your team, not what's trendy.

The projects on this site represent actual client work with verified time savings. Every automation I build is documented and designed to be understood by non-technical staff.
