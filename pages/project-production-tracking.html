Production Tracking Automation

Publishing house

Date: Nov 2025
Build Time: 4 hours
Saves: 14 days/year

Goal: Identify repetitive manual tasks within the organisation and automate them to reduce costs and save time, allowing staff to focus on higher-value work.

Book status information lived in five different spreadsheets. Checking if a book would meet its deadline meant searching multiple files and asking several people.

I built a single tracker showing every book's stage, owner, and deadline. Traffic lights flag at-risk books, progress bars show targets.

Two hours per week of chasing status information eliminated. Chaos replaced with clarity.
